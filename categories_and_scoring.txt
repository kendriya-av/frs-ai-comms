Judging Panel:
•	2-3 judges from VP/SVP level
•	Mix of business and technical leadership
•	Consider including someone from each major department
Presentation Format:
•	Total time per team: 20 minutes
o	12 minutes: Presentation
o	5 minutes: Demo
o	3 minutes: Q&A

Judging Categories & Scoring:
Category 1: Business Value & Impact (25 points)
Does this solution address a real central bank need?
Criteria	Points	Comment
Clear problem definition	5	
Potential impact on efficiency/effectiveness	10	
Feasibility of implementation	5	
Scalability potential	5	
Scoring Guide:
•	5 = Exceptional, transformative potential
•	4 = Strong business case
•	3 = Moderate value
•	2 = Limited value
•	1 = Unclear value

Category 2: Technical Innovation & Quality (25 points)
How well did they leverage AI/ML and AWS capabilities?
Criteria	Points	Comment
Appropriate use of AI/ML techniques	8	
Technical architecture quality	7	
Use of AWS services	5	
Code quality and documentation	5	
Scoring Guide:
•	Consider innovation within team's skill level
•	Reward creative solutions over complex ones
•	Technical feasibility matters

Category 3: User Experience & Design (20 points)
Is the solution intuitive and useful?
Criteria	Points	Comment
Interface design and usability	8	
Clarity of AI outputs/explanations	7	
Demonstration quality	5	
Scoring Guide:
•	Does it solve the user's problem elegantly?
•	Can non-technical users understand and use it?
•	Are AI decisions explainable?

Category 4: Teamwork & Execution (15 points)
How well did the team work together?
Criteria	Points	Comment
Cross-functional collaboration	5	
Presentation quality and clarity	5	
Project management (completing in 10 hours)	5	
Scoring Guide:
•	Evidence of diverse skills utilized
•	Clear communication
•	Realistic scope management

Category 5: AI Literacy & Learning (15 points)
Did the team demonstrate growth in AI understanding?
Criteria	Points	Comment
Understanding of AI capabilities and limitations	8	
Responsible AI considerations (bias, privacy, ethics)	7	
Scoring Guide:
•	This is about learning, not just building
•	Reward thoughtful consideration of AI implications
•	Acknowledge learning journey, especially for beginners

Total: 100 points
Award Categories:
1.	🏆 Grand Winner (Highest overall score)
2.	💡 Most Innovative Solution (Judges' special recognition)
3.	🎯 Best Business Impact (Highest Category 1 score)
4.	🤝 Best Team Collaboration (Peer-nominated, optional)
5.	📚 Best Learning Journey (Most growth in AI literacy)
